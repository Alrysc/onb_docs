# Playing Open Net Battle

This section of the website goes over setting up ONB, as well as things such as 
downloading mods, joining servers, configuring controls, and navigating menus. 
Beyond that, there are also some guides to help you understand some community 
lingo that'saround, such as what people mean when they say your mods aren't 
whitelisted, or what a "Special" is.

There will be some helpful pictures, too. These will be centered around 
Windows, but mostly will apply to other downloads.

These pages are ordered mostly chronologically, based on when you'll run into 
the topic. Reading top to bottom is recommended, but feel free to jump around.
